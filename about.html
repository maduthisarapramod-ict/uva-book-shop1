Our Story

Welcome to Uva Book Shop, your premier destination for books and stationery items in Badulla, Sri Lanka. We are dedicated to providing students, teachers, and book lovers with the best educational materials, high-quality stationery, and an extensive collection of local and international books.

Nestled in the beautiful hills of the Uva province, our shop aims to foster a love for reading and learning within our community. Whether you are looking for school essentials, university textbooks, or a cozy novel to read, we have something special for everyone.

Why Choose Us?

  • Affordable prices on all stationery and books.
  • Friendly and supportive customer service.
  • Easy and convenient ordering via WhatsApp.
  • Located in the heart of Badulla with a beautiful, welcoming environment.
